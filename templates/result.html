Sobel Edge Detection

Sobel edge detection highlights areas with sharp changes in pixel intensity, indicating the presence of edges. Abnormal or inconsistent edge patterns may suggest manipulations or artifacts introduced during the deepfake generation process.

Frequency Visualization

Frequency visualization reveals the distribution of frequency components in the image. Anomalies or inconsistencies in the frequency domain can indicate the presence of deepfake manipulations, as the generation process may introduce artifacts or unnatural frequency patterns.

Local Binary Patterns (LBP) Features

Local Binary Patterns (LBP) capture local texture patterns in the image. LBP features can help identify micro-patterns and textures that may be indicative of deepfake manipulations, as the synthesis process can introduce subtle irregularities or inconsistencies in the local texture patterns.

Gabor Features

Gabor features capture oriented frequency information and are sensitive to specific patterns and textures. They can help identify discrepancies or anomalies in the texture and orientation patterns that may arise from the deepfake generation process.
